About Nu Restaurant

The Story Behind the Authentic Flavor

Nu Restaurant is a modern culinary destination focused on delivering high-quality traditional Ethiopian meals with speed, convenience, and unforgettable flavor. Our mission is to combine fresh, local ingredients, great service, and innovative technology.

We give customers the best dining experience both in-store and through our mobile delivery app — from our kitchen to your door in record time.
