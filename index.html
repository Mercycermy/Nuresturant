About Nu Restaurant

Our Story & Heritage

The name "Nu" encapsulates a profound and evocative message, carrying a significant meaning and purpose. Our aspiration is to provide nourishment for both the body and the mind, while celebrating Ethiopia and the essence of Ethiopianism.

We aim to redefine misconceptions about nutrition by reconnecting it with our authentic traditional culture. Our major focus is to honor and elevate the profound wisdom embedded in our culinary heritage.

VisionTo serve food that truly represents us
MissionTo equally nourish both mind and body
Restore HeritageRestore Ethiopia's indigenous culinary practices
Promote & PreserveTraditional food systems rooted in heritage
