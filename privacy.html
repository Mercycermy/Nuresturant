Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 15, 2026

1. Introduction

Nu Restaurant ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you use our website and mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect personal information such as your name, contact details, and delivery address to facilitate your orders. We also collect usage data to improve our service delivery and application performance.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to process orders, provide logistics updates, and enhance your overall culinary experience. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

4. Data Security

We implement rigorous security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. All transactions are processed through secure digital gateways.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact our support team at support@nurestaurant.com or via the contact section on our main page.
