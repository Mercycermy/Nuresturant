Help Center

How can we assist you today?

How do I place an order?

You can place an order through our mobile application, available for download on the App Store and Google Play. Simply browse our menu, select your preferred dishes, and follow the checkout prompts.

What are the available payment methods?

We accept various digital payment methods including Telebirr and CBE Birr, as well as cash on delivery for your convenience.

Can I track my delivery in real-time?

Yes, once your order is confirmed and dispatched, you can monitor the real-time location of our courier directly within the Nu Restaurant mobile application.

Do you offer catering services?

Nu Restaurant provides catering for corporate events and private gatherings. Please reach out to our support team via the contact form on our main page for specialized inquiries.

Still have questions?

Our support team is available to assist you with any inquiries or feedback.
